Privacy Policy

How your personal information is collected, used, and protected.

1. Who we are

Halo Glow Beauty is a beauty studio based at 85 Main Road, Muizenberg, 7945, South Africa. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you book or enquire.

2. What information we collect

We may collect your name, contact number, email address, and any information you choose to share about your preferred treatments or availability.

3. How we use your information

We use your details only to respond to enquiries, confirm or manage bookings, and send essential appointment information. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. WhatsApp and email

When you contact us via WhatsApp or email, your messages are processed by those services under their own privacy terms. We keep these conversations only as long as reasonably necessary for client care and record-keeping.

5. Data retention

We retain booking and enquiry information for as long as needed for accounting, legal, or customer service purposes, after which it is securely deleted where possible.

6. Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information where applicable under South African law. To do so, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact us

For any privacy questions, you can contact Halo Glow Beauty via WhatsApp on 073 736 8023 or via the contact form on our website.
